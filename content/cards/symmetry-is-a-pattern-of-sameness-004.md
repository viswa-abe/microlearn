## Idea
Symmetry means: you can **transform something and it looks the same**. This isn't just about mirrors. Rotating a square 90 degrees gives the same shape. Translating a wallpaper pattern gives the same design. Every symmetry is a transformation that preserves structure.

## Example
The equation `x² + y² = 1` (a circle). Swap x and y -- still true. Negate x -- still true. Rotate by any angle -- still true. The circle has *infinite* symmetry, which is why it shows up everywhere in nature.

## Probe
A square has exactly 8 symmetries (4 rotations + 4 reflections). A circle has infinite. What shape has exactly 1 symmetry -- only the identity (doing nothing)?
